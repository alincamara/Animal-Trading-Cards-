Dogs: the adorable domestic pet beloved by millions. The dog is one of the most popular pets in the world -- it is, of course, commonly referred to as “man’s best friend.”

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 1 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 10 – 13 years
  • Habitat: Outside or in theouse

Dogs are also often employed as service animals and emotional support animals. Famous internet dogs include Marnie, Boo, Tuna, Toast, Manny the Frenchie, and Doug the Pug.
